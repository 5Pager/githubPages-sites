    What becomes possible after your check-up

    Once you have clarity about your smile, these are the treatments that can help you achieve your goals.

    Cosmetic Veneers

    Smile Transformation

    Ultra-thin porcelain shells that transform the shape, color, and alignment of your teeth. Perfect for addressing chips, gaps, staining, or creating your ideal smile shape.

    What they achieve:

    • Instantly straighter-looking teeth
    • Perfect color and brightness
    • Natural-looking results
    • Long-lasting transformation (15+ years)

    The process:

    2-3 appointments over 2-3 weeks. We design your new smile digitally before any treatment begins, so you'll see exactly what to expect.

    Ideal for:

    People who want a complete smile transformation but don't want to wait for orthodontics.

    Discuss Veneers

    Invisalign

    Teeth Straightening

    Clear, removable aligners that gradually straighten your teeth without traditional braces. Nearly invisible and designed for adult lifestyles.

    What they achieve:

    • Straighter teeth without metal braces
    • Removable for eating and cleaning
    • More comfortable than traditional braces
    • Predictable results with digital planning

    The process:

    12-18 months typically. You'll change aligners every 1-2 weeks, with check-ups every 6-8 weeks to monitor progress.

    Ideal for:

    Adults who want straighter teeth but prefer a discreet, flexible approach to treatment.

    Explore Invisalign

    Professional Whitening

    Brightness Enhancement

    Safe, effective whitening that's tailored to your teeth and lifestyle. Choice of in-practice power whitening or take-home systems.

    What they achieve:

    • Noticeably whiter teeth in days or weeks
    • Safe for enamel with professional monitoring
    • Customized to avoid sensitivity
    • Long-lasting results with maintenance

    The process:

    Single 90-minute appointment for power whitening, or take-home trays you use for 1-2 weeks at your convenience.

    Ideal for:

    People with healthy teeth who want to enhance their natural brightness safely and effectively.

    Learn About Whitening

    Dental Implants

    Tooth Replacement

    Permanent replacement for missing teeth that look, feel, and function like natural teeth. The closest thing to growing a new tooth.

    What they achieve:

    • Permanent solution for missing teeth
    • Preserve jawbone and facial structure
    • No impact on neighboring teeth
    • Eat, speak, and smile with confidence

    The process:

    3-6 months total. Implant placement, healing period, then custom crown attachment. We guide you through every stage.

    Ideal for:

    People with missing teeth who want the most natural, permanent replacement option available.

    Discuss Implants

    General Dentistry

    Preventive Care

    Comprehensive preventive care to keep your teeth and gums healthy. The foundation of a lifetime of confident smiles.

    What's included:

    • Professional hygiene appointments
    • Fillings using tooth-colored materials
    • Gum disease treatment and prevention
    • Oral health education and guidance

    The approach:

    Regular 6-month appointments with additional visits as needed. We focus on preventing problems before they become serious.

    Ideal for:

    Everyone! The foundation of good oral health that supports any cosmetic goals you might have.
