Is whitening right for you?

Our £95 consultation determines if whitening will achieve your desired results.

Great candidates have:

  • • Healthy teeth and gums
  • • Yellow or brown staining
  • • Realistic expectations
  • • No recent dental work on front teeth
  • • No severe tooth sensitivity

Whitening won't help with:

  • • Grey staining from medications
  • • Crowns, veneers, or fillings
  • • Severely damaged teeth
  • • Active gum disease
  • • Pregnant or breastfeeding patients
