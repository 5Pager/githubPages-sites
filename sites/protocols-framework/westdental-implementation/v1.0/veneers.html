What are porcelain veneers?

Ultra-thin shells of medical-grade porcelain, custom-designed to cover the front surface of your teeth.

Perfect for treating:

  • Chipped or cracked teeth

    Restore damaged teeth to their original beauty

  • Permanent stains or discoloration

    Cover stains that whitening can't address

  • Gaps between teeth

    Close small to medium gaps instantly

  • Misshapen or worn teeth

    Restore proper shape and proportion

  • Minor alignment issues

    Create the appearance of straighter teeth

Why choose porcelain?

  • • Light-reflecting properties match natural teeth
  • • Stain-resistant surface stays bright
  • • Biocompatible and gum-friendly
  • • Long-lasting with proper care (10-15 years)
  • • Minimal tooth preparation required

Before we begin your veneers

Our £95 consultation determines if veneers are right for you and your smile goals.

Veneers might be perfect if:

  • • You have healthy teeth and gums
  • • You want to improve your smile's appearance
  • • You don't grind your teeth at night
  • • You maintain good oral hygiene
  • • You're committed to regular dental visits

Alternatives we might suggest:

  • • Teeth whitening for stain removal
  • • Composite bonding for minor chips
  • • Invisalign for alignment issues
  • • Crowns for severely damaged teeth
