What is Invisalign?

A series of custom-made, clear plastic aligners that gradually move your teeth into the desired position.

Perfect for treating:

  • Crowded teeth

    Create space for overlapping teeth

  • Gaps between teeth

    Close unwanted spaces

  • Overbite or underbite

    Correct bite relationship

  • Crooked teeth

    Align rotated or tilted teeth

  • Crossbite issues

    Improve bite function

Why choose Invisalign?

  • • Nearly invisible - most people won't notice
  • • Removable for eating and cleaning
  • • No food restrictions
  • • Comfortable with no metal irritation
  • • Predictable results with 3D planning
  • • Typically faster than traditional braces

Am I suitable for Invisalign?

Our £95 consultation includes a thorough assessment to determine if Invisalign can achieve your goals.

Good candidates typically have:

  • • Mild to moderate alignment issues
  • • All permanent teeth present
  • • Good oral hygiene habits
  • • Commitment to wearing aligners 20+ hours daily
  • • Realistic expectations about results
  • • No active gum disease

Invisalign may not be suitable for:

  • • Severe bite problems
  • • Large gaps or major crowding
  • • Teeth requiring significant rotation
  • • Active tooth decay or gum disease
  • • Poor oral hygiene
  • • Inability to commit to wearing aligners
