Why professional hygiene matters

Even with excellent home care, professional cleaning removes deposits that brushing and flossing simply can't reach.

What we remove:

  • Tartar and calculus

    Hard deposits that cause gum disease

  • Plaque bacteria

    Sticky film that leads to decay

  • Surface stains

    From coffee, tea, wine, and smoking

  • Food debris

    Particles trapped in hard-to-reach areas

Health benefits:

  • • Prevents gum disease and tooth loss
  • • Reduces risk of heart disease
  • • Prevents bad breath
  • • Early detection of problems
  • • Maintains natural tooth whiteness
  • • Improves overall health

Maintaining your results

Professional cleaning works best when combined with excellent daily oral hygiene.

Daily essentials:

  • • Brush twice daily with fluoride toothpaste
  • • Floss daily to remove plaque between teeth
  • • Use antibacterial mouthwash
  • • Replace toothbrush every 3 months
  • • Limit sugary and acidic foods
  • • Stay hydrated with water

Professional schedule:

Every 6 months

Standard cleaning and check-up for most patients

Every 3-4 months

If you have gum disease or are prone to buildup

As needed

Stain removal or problem areas
