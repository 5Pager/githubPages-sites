When should you book a check-up?

Regular check-ups are important, but some situations require immediate attention.

Book urgently if you have:

  • • Persistent tooth pain
  • • Swelling in your mouth or face
  • • Bleeding gums that won't stop
  • • Broken or loose teeth
  • • Lost fillings or crowns
  • • Bad taste or smell that won't go away
