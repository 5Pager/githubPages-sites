What are dental implants?

Titanium posts surgically placed into the jawbone that act as artificial tooth roots, supporting crowns, bridges, or dentures.

Why choose implants?

  • Look and feel natural

    Indistinguishable from your own teeth

  • Preserve bone health

    Prevent jawbone deterioration

  • Last a lifetime

    With proper care, implants can last decades

  • No impact on adjacent teeth

    Unlike bridges, neighbouring teeth stay untouched

  • Eat anything you want

    Full chewing power restored

Implant options:

  • • Single tooth implant - replace one missing tooth
  • • Multiple implants - replace several teeth
  • • Implant-supported bridge - replace multiple adjacent teeth
  • • All-on-4/All-on-6 - full arch replacement
  • • Implant-retained dentures - secure loose dentures

Your implant journey

A carefully planned process that ensures the best possible outcome for your new teeth.

1

Assessment & Planning (£95)

We use advanced 3D imaging to assess your jawbone and plan your implant placement precisely. Not everyone is suitable for implants - we'll provide honest advice about your options.

  • 3D CBCT scan and assessment
  • Bone density evaluation
  • Treatment planning and timeline

Comprehensive Planning

2

Implant Placement

The titanium implant is surgically placed into the jawbone using precise guidance. This procedure is typically done under local anaesthetic and is more comfortable than most patients expect.

"I was amazed how painless the procedure was. Dr. Jandoo made me feel completely at ease throughout." — Robert M.

Precision Placement

3

Healing & Integration

Over 3-6 months, the implant fuses with your jawbone in a process called osseointegration. During this time, you may have a temporary crown if the implant is in a visible area.

Healing timeline:

  • • First week: Initial healing and comfort
  • • 2-4 weeks: Soft tissue healing complete
  • • 3-6 months: Bone integration complete
  • • Ready for permanent crown

Natural Integration

4

Crown Placement

Once healing is complete, we attach your custom-made crown. This is designed to match your natural teeth perfectly in both form and function.

"Nobody can tell which tooth is the implant. It looks and feels exactly like my natural teeth." — Helen K.

Perfect Crown

Are you suitable for implants?

Our £95 consultation includes a full assessment to determine if implants are the best solution for you.

Good candidates have:

  • • Good overall health
  • • Adequate bone density
  • • Healthy gums
  • • Non-smoker (or willing to quit)
  • • Commitment to oral hygiene
  • • Realistic expectations

Success rate

Dental implants have a 95-98% success rate when placed by experienced practitioners.

We may need alternatives if you have:

  • • Insufficient bone volume
  • • Uncontrolled diabetes
  • • Heavy smoking habit
  • • Recent cancer treatment
  • • Certain medications
  • • Active gum disease
